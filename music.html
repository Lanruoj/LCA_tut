Music

Music is a force within my life that has likely had the most profound effect on shaping who I am and how I feel. Ever since I discovered my sister's "alternative" music collection in early highschool, I've been continually exploring and discovering new music and sharing it to whoever would listen (not many, as it turns out).

Here are some of my favourite albums:

  1. Stolen Violin, Temperate Touch, Tropical Tears (2013)
  2. King Krule, Man Alive! (2020)
  3. Jordan Ireland With Purple Orchestra, Jordan Ireland With Purple Orchestra (2017)
  4. Arthur Russell, World of Echo (1986)
  5. Sunbeam Sound Machine, Wonderer (2014)
  6. Amen Dunes, Love (2014)
  7. Connan Mockasin, Forever Dolphin Love (2011)
  8. Soda Eaves, Murray, Darling (2016)
